## Full description

**Your body, drawn fresh every morning.**
Lifeline turns your signals — heart rate, HRV, sleep, movement — into one
daily vitality score, a biological "Lifeline Age", readiness, and the habits
that actually move them. Everything is computed **on your device**, from rules
our servers publish. We never see a heartbeat.

**Compete on health itself.**
Log your vitality score to the global Arena: six leagues from Bronze to Apex,
weekly seasons, streaks and XP. Rivals see your handle, league, and score —
never a single biometric. It's a leaderboard the server ranks blind.

**A vault we cannot open.**
Journals and doctor's lab results are encrypted before they leave your phone.
Labs are plotted against healthy reference ranges — on-device.

**Every source, one readiness.**
Apple Health, Health Connect, and Whoop fuse into a single readiness score,
weighted over whatever you've connected.

**A coach that forgets.**
Ask anything. Messages pass through a privacy proxy that strips your identity
before the model reads them, and nothing is stored.

**Plans**
- **Lifeline** — free forever: your daily portrait, one source, 7-day history,
  leaderboard viewing.
- **Lifeline Pro** — every source fused, biomarker tracking, unlimited history
  and coaching, weekly-season competition, ad-free.
- **Lifeline Elite** — everything in Pro, plus beta releases and early features.

No account. No e-mail. No password. Your device *is* your identity, proven by
hardware attestation.
