Privacy Policy

Lifeline — the zero-knowledge longevity engine

The short version

Lifeline is built so that we cannot read your health data — even if we wanted to. Your biometrics are read and processed on your device. What reaches our servers is either ciphertext we cannot decrypt or a single opaque score you chose to share.

What stays on your device

  • All raw health signals (heart rate, HRV, sleep, steps, workouts) read from Apple Health, Health Connect, or connected wearables.
  • All insight computation: your vitality score, Lifeline Age, readiness, correlations, and circadian windows are calculated locally from rules our servers publish.
  • Your encryption keys. Vault documents are encrypted with keys that never leave your device.

What our servers store

  • Encrypted vault documents — ciphertext, an initialization vector, and an authentication tag. We cannot decrypt them.
  • Your Arena profile — a pseudonymous handle you chose, your league, level, streak, and the opaque 0–100 vitality score you submit. No biometric can be reconstructed from it.
  • A device attestation record — a random device identifier and a public key, established via Apple App Attest (iOS).
  • Your account — an email address (or an Apple/Google sign-in identifier) and, for email sign-up, a salted password hash. This unlocks access and enables recovery across devices; it holds no encryption keys and no health data.
  • Subscription state — your plan tier and billing references (see Payments).

AI coach

Coach messages are routed through our privacy proxy, which strips your IP address, device identifiers, and all metadata before the model sees a word. Conversations are not stored and are never used for training. On supported devices you can download an on-device model, after which the coach runs entirely on your device and no message leaves it at all.

Payments

Subscriptions purchased in our iOS and Android apps are processed by Apple and Google respectively; on the web they are processed by Stripe. Card details never touch Lifeline's servers. We store only the plan you're on, its status, and provider references needed to manage it. Payment providers process your data under their own policies.

Third-party connections

Connecting Apple Health or Health Connect grants on-device read access only — our servers just record that you consented. Connecting Whoop uses OAuth2; the access token is encrypted at rest on our servers and is never returned to any client.

What we never do

  • No selling or sharing of personal data. Ever.
  • No advertising identifiers, no cross-app tracking.
  • No third-party analytics SDKs in the app.

Your controls

Delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete account. This permanently erases your account and everything tied to it on our servers — encrypted documents, your Arena profile, subscription records, and device registration — and wipes your local data. Resetting your device identity instead severs the link to server-side data without deleting the account. You can also disconnect any source, or cancel your subscription, at any time in the app.
